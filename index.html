marketing tools for destinations

We develop marketing tools for the tourism industry. We provide our products to destinations around the world.

SOCIAL WIDGET

Because the best travel recommendations comes from friends.

A guide where visitors easily get answer to which places that are most popular at a destination, both from the crowd and from friends. The guide is placed as a widget on destinations existing webpages.

VISITOR'S GUIDE

For mobiles, tablets & touch screens

We make tourism destinations information available on touchscreens, from mobiles and tablets to large touchscreens. Indoor or outdoor, hardware and software.

No expensive licenses or programming costs, we use existing databases and CMS. Responsive design, it looks great on all screens of any size, format and resolution.

LOCATION ANALYTICS

A business intelligence software for everyday use.

We offer marketing analysis in real time, where you continuously can see which places are most popular, least popular, most active and most engaging. You can see in real time where your visitors check in, tags pictures, write reviews, and meet up with friends.

You can follow travelers from the very beginning, from the time they begin to inquire in to visit your destination, what they do during the stay, see what they write about places, and what impressions they got after leaving the destination.

This information helps you improve and modify marketing communications for the destination.
